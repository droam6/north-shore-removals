What's Covered

Full-Service Residential Moves Across the North Shore

Moving house is one of the most stressful things a family can take on. North Shore Removals exists to make it the opposite. Whether you're shifting from a one-bedroom unit in Chatswood to a four-bedroom federation home in Pymble, or moving across the road in Mosman, we plan, pack, lift, drive and place every item with the care of a team that has done this thousands of times. Our crews know the North Shore intimately — the steep driveways in Killara, the strata rules in North Sydney, the narrow lanes around Cremorne — and we factor all of it into how we approach your day.

What's Included in a Home Move

Every house move includes a full pre-move walkthrough so we can quote accurately and plan logistics. On the day, our team arrives with furniture blankets, trolleys, straps, shrink wrap and tools to disassemble and reassemble beds, dining tables, wardrobes and any flat-pack items. Fragiles like artwork, mirrors, glassware and electronics are wrapped and packed individually before they go on the truck. We protect floors and walls in both the origin and destination homes with runners and corner guards. Heavy items — pianos, safes, pool tables, large fridges — are handled with specialist equipment and trained crew.

How a Typical Day Runs

For a 3-bedroom home, expect us to arrive between 7–8am with a 2- or 3-man team and a fully equipped truck. We'll do a quick walkthrough of what's coming with us, then start loading the largest items first to maximise truck space. Most full-house moves take 4–6 hours all in. We drive carefully, use GPS-tracked vehicles so you can follow along, and unload room-by-room so unpacking is manageable. We don't leave until every item is exactly where you want it and you've done a final walkthrough.

Common North Shore Home Types We Move

The North Shore has a real mix of housing stock. We regularly handle California bungalows and federation homes in Killara and Roseville, mid-century homes around Lindfield and Gordon, and modern apartments in North Sydney and Neutral Bay. Whether your move involves multi-storey access, lift bookings, narrow stairwells or restricted parking, we've planned for it before.

Insurance & Peace of Mind

Every home move is fully insured under our transit and public liability cover. We carry comprehensive insurance on all goods in our care from the moment they leave your old place to the moment they're set down in the new one. If you're considering a different type of move, see our apartment moves, office relocations or single-item moves — or check our transparent pricing for crew sizes and rates.
