What's Covered

Minimise Downtime With Experienced Commercial Movers

An office move is operations-critical work disguised as logistics. Get it wrong and your business loses revenue every hour the phones aren't answered. North Shore Removals plans commercial relocations as projects, not jobs — with site walkthroughs, written sequencing plans, and crews that have moved everything from boutique law firms in North Sydney to creative agencies on Crows Nest and clinical practices in Chatswood. The brief is always the same: minimise downtime, protect equipment, leave the new space ready to work in by Monday morning.

After-Hours & Weekend Moves

Most of our office moves run after-hours, on Saturdays, or in long weekends to avoid disrupting trading. We coordinate building access with both old and new building managers, secure loading dock bookings, and obtain after-hours security clearance. Our crews work in shifts where required and we scale truck and team size to compress the move into the available window. For larger projects we will run a phased move so critical departments stay operational throughout.

IT Equipment, Files & Specialised Items

Workstations, monitors, server racks, multi-function printers and cabling all get individual attention. We label, photograph and crate where appropriate so re-installation in the new space is straightforward. Filing cabinets are kept upright and locked; if you need files boxed and barcoded for compliance, we can do that too. Boardroom tables, sit-stand desks, ergonomic chairs and reception furniture are wrapped in furniture blankets and transported on trolleys to avoid scratches and dings.

Building Coordination & Compliance

Most North Shore commercial buildings have strict moving rules — lift bookings, dock-loading windows, COI (certificate of insurance) requirements, OH&S sign-off. We handle all of this paperwork before move day, sending COIs to the building manager, booking lifts, registering vehicles and crew, and complying with strata or owners-corp moving hours. We are public-liability insured to the levels most premium buildings require.

How We Quote

Office moves are quoted only after a site visit. We need to see the layout, identify any unusual items (server racks, safes, oversized items), confirm access at both ends, and understand your timing window. After the visit we send a written quote with crew size, truck count, hours, and any add-ons. See our home moves, apartment moves, single-item moves or general pricing.
